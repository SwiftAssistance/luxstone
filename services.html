Our Premium Services

From the initial groundworks to the final polished finish, our team ensures every project meets the Luxestone standard of excellence. Use the tabs below to explore our core specialisms.

Luxury Driveways

The perfect entrance to your home. We specialize in high-load bearing surfaces that combine kerb appeal with practical durability.

Resin Bound

Our most popular choice. A smooth, permeable surface available in stunning blends like golden quartz and amber. It features a sharp, high-contrast finish that remains puddle-free thanks to its SUDS compliance.

  • Weed resistant
  • Smooth, anti-slip finish
  • Fully permeable

Block Paving

Timeless and versatile. We utilize high-end construction materials in charcoal and grey tones, creating clean lines and immaculate herringbone patterns laid on a solid concrete base.

  • High load-bearing
  • Wide color range
  • Easy maintenance

Gravel & Shingle

A rustic yet neat pathway solution. We use decorative golden shingle with stabilization grids to prevent migration, ensuring a firm surface that captures the soft daylight beautifully.

  • Security deterrent
  • Natural drainage
  • Rustic aesthetic

Patios & Paving

Extend your living space outdoors. Our bespoke patio installations create the perfect setting for relaxation and entertainment.

Porcelain Paving

Experience sleek, minimalist design with large-format porcelain tiles. Non-porous and scratch-resistant, they offer a smooth finish that looks stunning under evening garden lighting.

  • Stain & fade resistant
  • Modern clean lines
  • R11 Anti-slip rating

Indian Sandstone

Rich with natural character, featuring color variations of mint, fossil, and brown. The hand-cut edges and riven texture catch the sunlight, adding warmth to any garden.

  • Natural variations
  • Hand-cut or sawn
  • Ages beautifully

Restoration & Cleaning

Bring your old patio back to life. We offer professional jet washing, sealing, and re-pointing services to restore the original beauty of your paving.

  • Pressure cleaning
  • Resin pointing
  • Protective sealing

Landscaping & Groundworks

Complete garden transformation and essential structural foundations. We handle everything from the dirt up.

Hard Landscaping

Beautifully constructed retaining walls using red brick and wooden sleepers. We create raised flower beds, steps, and levels that bring professional structure to your outdoor space.

  • Brickwork & Walls
  • Sleepers & Beds
  • Steps & Levels

Turfing & Fencing

We install freshly laid rolled lawn turf for vibrant green stripes. Combined with sturdy closeboard or panel fencing, we create a bright, airy, and secure garden atmosphere.

  • Premium Rolawn Turf
  • Artificial Grass
  • Security Fencing

Groundworks & Drainage

Professional engineering and stability are at the core of our groundworks. We handle neat trenching, foundations, and drainage to ensure your project stands the test of time.

  • Site Clearance
  • Concrete Bases
  • Aco Drains
